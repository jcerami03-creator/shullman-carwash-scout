BlueWave™ Is a Value Retailer. BlueWave™’s car washes are long conveyor
express (LCX) facilities. Of over 200 car washes in the Houston metro, literally
only a handful are state-of-the-art LCX car washes. That is not to say that LCX’s
are a new kind of car wash. LCX’s are in operation in many markets. In Portland,
Oregon for example, they dominate the market, displacing all but a few full-service
car washes. The reason is simple. They offer the consumer a compelling value
differential. A full-service car wash needs 20 people to wash 50 cars in an hour.
An LCX with only 2 people can wash 150 cars in an hour, and get the cars as
clean and as dry as a full-service wash. Where a full service wash takes 15 to 25
minutes, and typically costs the consumer $15 to $25, an LCX produces a clean,
dry car exterior in less than 3 minutes and is highly profitable charging $5 or less.
Conveniently, the customer does not get out of the car during the process. Upon
exiting the car wash, the customer is offered free vacuums when he or she wants
to take the time to clean the inside of the car.

BlueWave™ Addresses Huge Pent-Up Demand. There are over 200 million
consumer owned cars in America. According to extensive consumer research done
by the International Car Wash Association, fewer than half of those cars are ever
washed in a professional car wash. They are washed in their owners’ driveways
or not at all. The predominant reasons given by customers for home washing
are concerns about price and damage to the car's finish. BlueWave™ does away
with the price issue: $4.99 for a clean, dry car. It also offers a car wash that is
safer for a car's finish than hand-washing. BlueWave guarantees against damage.
BlueWave’s™ marketing campaigns educate consumers in its trade areas to the
value and safety it offers, targeting both existing car wash users and home washers.
BlueWave is winning over large numbers of customers now using full service, gas
station, and self-service car washes, as well as large numbers of home washers.

BlueWave™ is an Environmentally Responsible Retailer. Washing a car at
BlueWave™ requires about 25% of the water that washing your car in your driveway
requires, That is because BlueWave™ reclaims and reuses most of its water. Also,
BlueWave™ uses 100% biodegradable soaps and waxes.

is a “mom-and-pop” business today. There are nearly 100,000 car washes in the
United States. Some 90% are owned by individuals or entities that own 3 or fewer.

stores, There are only 3 dozen car wash companies that own more than a dozen
apres. Only about 10%. of all car washes are LCX’s. Most owners of car washes
